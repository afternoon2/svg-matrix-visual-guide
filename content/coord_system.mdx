Here you can see transformation in action.

- Drag the root rectangle (orange stroke) and observe changes in the transformed figure (opaque orange background)
- Click on the root rectangle's corners to see their transformation paths
- Hover on them to preview their coordinates.
- Hover on transformed rectangle's corners to preview their coordinates, too
- Hover on transformation path's contact points to see each step's coordinates.

You can also switch between the rectangle's points using the Point Switch on the bottom right side.
